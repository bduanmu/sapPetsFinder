'''Pets''' are the player's units in Super Auto Pets. Each type of pet has a unique ability, base Health, and base Attack. Pets are bought from the [[Shop]] for 3 {{IconSAP|Gold}} each, and placed in the player's team. The player's team can only have 5 pets at once. The player's pets also have a limit of 50 for {{IconSAP|attack}}, {{IconSAP|health}}, and {{IconSAP|mana}}, except for the {{IconSAP|Behemoth}}. (for the former two)

Pets in a player's party can be:
* Repositioned in the team.
* Sold for 1 {{IconSAP|Gold}} per sell value (normally its level).
* Merged with other pets of the same type to gain {{IconSAP|Experience}}, and potentially level up.
* Fed [[Food]] to give them benefits like stat increase, [[Food Perks]], or other.

Pet tiers unlock for purchase every other turn until all tiers are unlocked, with tier 1 available on turn 1 to tier 6 opening up on turn 11. The formula is tier X being unlockable in turn (2X-1). All Pets from all previous tiers of the highest tier can still appear.

In battle, your side of Pets attack in order from right to left with the enemy attacking from left to right.

Some pets have abilities that work well with other pets. See [[Pet Archetypes]].

== List of Pets ==
There are a total of 360 pets. 60 pets are in the '''{{IconSAP|Turtle Pack|image=Turtle Icon.png}}''', 29 new pets in {{IconSAP|Custom Packs|image=Tiger Icon.png}}. 60 pets are in the '''{{IconSAP|Unicorn Pack|image=Unicorn Icon.png}}''' itself while 24 new pets are exclusive to {{IconSAP|Custom Packs|image=Unicorn Custom Exclusive Icon.png}} but come within the Unicorn Pack. 62 new pets are in the '''{{IconSAP|Golden Pack|image=Golden Retriever Icon.png}}'''. 59 new pets are in the '''{{IconSAP|Star Pack|image=Starfish Icon.png}}''' itself while 18 are exclusive to {{IconSAP|Custom Packs|image=Star Custom Exclusive Icon.png}} but come within the Star Pack. 48 new pets are in the {{IconSAP|Puppy Pack|image=Puppy Icon.png}}.

A pet is available for use in Custom Packs as long as you own its appropriate pack. All Pets except {{IconSAP|Strawberry}} pets and Jerboa are capable of being in the randomly generated Weekly Packs.
